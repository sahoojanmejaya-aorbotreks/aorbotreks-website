F. COOKIES AND SESSION DATA

Cookies

Aorbo Treks uses cookies to enhance your experience on our Website and to personalize advertisements displayed to you. The use of cookies by Aorbo Treks is in line with the practices followed by reputable online companies.

Cookies are small pieces of information stored by your browser on your device’s hard drive. They enable us to offer you a more efficient and tailored experience, allowing you to access our services with ease, such as logging in without needing to enter your username repeatedly (only your password is required). Cookies also help us display relevant advertisements to you or send offers via email based on your preferences and interests, provided you haven’t opted out of receiving such communications.

Additionally, cookies may be placed by our advertising partners or third-party advertising companies to help track the effectiveness of ads served on the Website. These cookies gather aggregated statistics to assist us in providing targeted advertisements for services that might interest you. The data collected through these cookies is anonymous and does not contain any Personal Information.

Most web browsers are set to accept cookies by default. However, you can modify your browser settings or use software tools to manage cookie preferences. We support your right to block cookies, especially from untrusted sites. Please note that blocking cookies from Aorbo Treks may affect certain features on the Website, potentially limiting your ability to use specific services smoothly.

Automatic Logging of Session Data

Each time you access the Website, your session data is logged. This session data may include details such as your IP address, the operating system you’re using, the browser type, and actions performed on the Website. This data helps us analyze user behavior, such as browsing patterns, frequency of visits, and duration of your stay on the site. Additionally, it aids in diagnosing issues with our servers and optimizing our systems.

Although this session data cannot personally identify you, it may help us determine the general location of your internet connection based on the IP address and identify your Internet Service Provider (ISP). This information is collected for analytical and technical purposes and does not link to your personal identity.
